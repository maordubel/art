About

The Dubel Team Art Department curates and privately places modern and contemporary works — early-twentieth-century European modernism, post-war abstraction, and contemporary mixed-media and sculpture. Each piece is selected for quality, rarity and lasting significance.

Every work is held in our care and offered with documented provenance and, where available, exhibition and publication history. Condition reports, additional images and shipping quotes are provided on request. Acquisitions and viewings are arranged privately, by appointment, for a discerning international clientele.
